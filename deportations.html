Arrests and Deportations (ToadVille Bay)
Note: this is an action that can be used in game.
About Arrests and Deportations

When a foriegn national commits a crime, they are arrested like everyone else, depending on the crime, they may be subjected to deportations. Being deported from ToadVille Bay can have a major impact on return. There are multipe types of deportations that can be used.

Types of Deportations
  • Depature: Must leave ToadVille Bay within 30 Days, with return at any time if requirements are met. (Reserved for minor crimes or overstays, depends on the legnth of overstays)
  • Exclusion: Must leave ToadVille Bay within 15 Days, with a bar for 1-5 Years
  • Expulsion (Expelled): Must leave ToadVille Bay within 5 Days, with a permanent bar from returing unless waived
What if I can't leave within the time frame?.

Notify INM about this, then have them settle a depature date.

What if I do not leave by the time?
  • Depending on the order you got, it'll be turned into an Explusion Order.
  • A nation-wide arrest warrant will be issued.
Note: Appealing an order must be done after depature..
