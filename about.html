Our Mission & Vision

ToadVille Bay is dedicated to making software that is easy to use, and to connect with others, software such as Videos Games. ToadVille Bay is founded by GhostVista, since July 3, 2019, GhostVista visioned software that you pay once and you get everything included, no microtransaction, DLCs, or subscriptions. ToadVille Bay is a game where you can have fun, the main focus being trying to complete campaign missions, with more modes. GhostVista also plans on making other software, such as TonnectDB, a forum style platform. ToadVille Bay isn't just a video game, but a community. We also make animations such as GhostVista Gets Grounded.
