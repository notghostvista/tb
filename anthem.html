ToadVille Bay National Anthem

"Oh ToadVille Bay"

Oh ToadVille Bay,
Land of our ancestors,
A ton of the stars,
The stars shine at the day.

The day comes by,
For our nation,
ToadVille, Our Land!,
From North to South

From East to West,
We the people stand with no fear,
People keep or land,
Strong safe and free!
Oh ToadVille Bay!
We stand for our people!
Oh ToadVille Bay!
We are ToadVillenese!

About Our Anthem

The ToadVille Bay anthem, "Oh ToadVille Bay," was composed to celebrate our unity, natural beauty, and shared values. It is performed at all official ceremonies and national celebrations.

When We Sing Our Anthem

  • National Day Celebrations
  • ToadVille Bay Games!
  • Rallies
  • Government Events
