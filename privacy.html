Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Data Collection

  • We only collect personal data when it is required by law, such as in response to government requests, subpoenas, or other legal obligations.
  • We do not collect unnecessary personal information. Only the minimum data required for compliance is retained.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics unrelated to legal compliance.
  • Data is collected by e-mail, Phone, and IP Address, given more when given a subpoena request.

2. Data Usage

  • Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.
  • Any data collected is used strictly for legal compliance and to fulfill obligations required by government authorities.
  • We do not use your data for any purpose other than what is required by law.

3. Data Protection

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
  • Access to your data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who are required to keep the information confidential.

4. Data Retention

  • We retain your data only as long as necessary to comply with legal obligations.
  • Once data is no longer required by law, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

5. No Misuse of Data

  • We have a zero-tolerance policy for misuse of personal data. Any misuse will result in strict action, including disciplinary measures for staff and reporting to authorities if required.
  • We regularly review our data handling practices to ensure compliance and prevent misuse.

6. Your Rights

  • You have the right to know what data is collected about you and how it is used.
  • If you have any questions or concerns about your data, you may contact us using the information provided on our website.

7. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website's contact page.
