GhostVista Gets Grounded (GVGG)
GhostVista Gets Grounded is a comedic animated web series created by GhostVista, following the misadventures of a fictionalized version of GhostVista as a troublemaking teen. The series is known for its humor, over-the-top scenarios, and satirical take on the "grounded video" genre popular on the internet.
Note: GhostVista Gets Grounded is not avaliable in the United States.
About the Series

In GVGG, GhostVista finds himself in all sorts of trouble, often resulting in being grounded by his parents for increasingly ridiculous reasons. The show blends classic cartoon slapstick with modern internet culture, making it a favorite among fans of parody and animation.

Key Features
  • Animated comedy series with episodic adventures
  • Satirical take on the "grounded" genre
  • Created, written, and voiced by GhostVista
  • Focuses on humor, creativity, and community in the ToadVille Bay universe
Episodes

Episodes are released on the ToadVille Bay website and may also be available on select video platforms. Each episode features GhostVista facing a new challenge or scheme, with plenty of laughs along the way.
