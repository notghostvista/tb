Note: This is a status within the ToadVille Bay Game
About Permanent Residents

A Permanent Resident is a person with permission to live, work, and study in ToadVille Bay permanently. Obtaining PR is a huge process.

How to get one
  • Have a invitation to apply for PR from INM
  • CLOSED: Upgrade from Temporary Residency (If you are Tempo Resident, please upgrade now as your status will be relinqushed at the expiry date and cannot be renewed due to Tempo Residency being discontintued)
  • Must have no criminal records.
What about temporary resident

Temporary Residency has been discontinued.

Do countries accept this document
  • Some countries in the ToadVille Bay Universe do, not all.
  • In a roleplay Discord Server, it was noted that "Great Union of Tajan" accepts it, in the ToadVille Bay Mapping Discord, most do accept such document.
Note: If you lie about your application in the pool of skilled workers, you will be deported.
